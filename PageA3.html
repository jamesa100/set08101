CAN I ESCAPE THIS DUNGEON! - ACT 1

As you pick up the sword a feeling of warmth flows through you body you begin to feel faster and stronger than before.
You continue to search the room further and notice the bones have all disappeared as you sense a dark presence nearby.
From across the room a skeleton warrior lunge at you. With this newfound power you thrust your sword at the monster,
swinging with all your might you land a critical hit and the skeleton is reduced to ash. As the dark presence fades you hear a
loud crash as the wall to your left collapses. You make your escape through this hole and pray for freedom. THE END
